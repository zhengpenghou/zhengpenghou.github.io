Our Team

Alan Li

CEO/Founder

Veteran compiler and virtual machine engineer. Previously Alan has worked for about 10 years in semiconductor companies such as IBM, MIPS, AMD, Huawei. His experience covers system software design, implementation and optimization in compilers, virtual machines and binary translators in homogeneous (CPU, DSP) and heterogeneous (GPU) computation. Alan was the maintainer of AMD's OpenCL driver for Apple, and the author of EVM's LLVM backend. Alan obtained his MSc in computing science from the University of Alberta, and BE of computer science from the University of Science and Technology of China.

Adrian Tong

CTO/Co-Founder

Veteran compiler engineer. Xin has extensive experience in language implementation and optimization. Previously, Xin has worked in multiple world-class compiler teams in Facebook, Apple and Intel. Xin wrote a significant part of the Swift optimizer during his time at Apple, most of the optimizations are still in use in Xcode after four years. In the past few years, Xin has helped Facebook transition from gcc to clang compiler. Particularly, Xin served as the technical lead in improving core compiler optimizations and link time optimization such that Facebook does not lose (or in some cases gain) performance while doing the toolchain transition. Xin obtained his MAsc and BAsc from the University of Toronto where he conducted research in compiler and computer architecture
